The painting is titeld "The Consul Louis Fauvel Painting" by Louis Dupré from 1819-20.
The painting represents Louis-Sébastien Fauvel (1753-1838) Consul of France in Greece in 1780-1822, who was commissioned to send antiquities to France. In the background the Acropolis in Athens can be seen, showing the painter's interest in antique monuments.
