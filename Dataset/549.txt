The painting is titeld "Paul Alexis Reading to Zola" by Paul Cézanne from 1869-70.
The painting, built up using effective light-dark and red-green contrasts, shows Cézanne's friend from his youth, Émile Zola, author of the Les Rougon-Macquart. The other figure is Zola's secretary Paul Alexis, a journalist, writer and friend of Cézanne from his time in Aix.
