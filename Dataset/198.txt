The painting is titeld "Miniature of Elise Wolf" by Moritz Michael Daffinger from c. 1830.
Moritz Michael Daffinger was one of Vienna's most successful portraitists in the early nineteenth century. He started his career at the Vienna porcelain factory, and later his domain became the portrait miniature. He portrayed the actors of Vienna, painted guest portraits for the albums of his wife, and became acquainted with Grillparzer who introduced him into Schubert's circle.
