The painting is titeld "Portrait of Henri Cordier" by Gustave Caillebotte from 1883.
Henri Cordier (1849-1925) was a French linguist, historian, ethnographer, author, editor and Orientalist. He was President of the Société de Géographie in Paris. He worked in China from 1869 to 1880, when he returned to France and became a lecturer on the geography and history of the Far East at the School of Oriental Languages in Paris.
