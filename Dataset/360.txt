The painting is titeld "View of the Falls at Tivoli" by Johannes Jakob Frey from 1840s.
Frey made frequent excursions into the Roman countryside, particularly in the summer months, making plein-air landscape sketches. The present view depicts the hill town of Tivoli and its famous waterfall. Tivoli was a favourite subject among artists, attracted by the spectacular falls and magnificent views over the surrounding countryside.
